A world

Welcome to Armony, a "perfect" city-world if you listen to the elite. You are T.I.T.O.R, an official working at the temporal agency. It's first day. Your goal? Use your Chronophone, a cassette reader that transports the wearer through time in order to do a simple task, to keep the order in the timeline. Your first mission starts in the 1930's new orleans, where you'll soon learn that your actions have consequences, big ones.

A narrative game

Gather information and do the right actions in this VR point and click. Using yarn spinner and complex dialog branching permits a full immersion in the story. You can see an example of level design approach in the figma here.
